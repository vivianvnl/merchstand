about me :D

nice to meet you, i'm vivian!

i'm 24f and my whole life revolves around a good book, movie, show, or music. i’m a media fanatic through and through. i spend most of my days alternating between reading and going to the movie theater and belt out my favorite screaming songs on the ride there. i grew up in a family of musicians, studied film, visual art, and literature in uni and always had a fascination with coding for art, so here we are!

i love viewing the world through the lens of contemporary romanticism, Call Me By Your Name (book, 2007, and movie, 2017), Frances Ha (movie, 2012), Boyscott's Goose Bumps (album, 2015), Perfect Days (movie, 2023), and Beautiful World, Where Are You by Sally Rooney (book, 2021) being my favorite examples so far on this.

this page is dedicated to reviewing media and ranting about allllll my faves. take my hand, let’s go!!!

come explore the world as i see it! it’s wacky, fun, and full of life (just like my name's meaning hehe). if u want to see my art/films check out my portfolio page :)
